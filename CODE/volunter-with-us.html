VOLUNTEER WITH US

Interested in helping our community?

If you are interested in volunteering your sewing skills to make masks for our community please contact the zipper (Maskateer organizer) in your town. Please provide your name, zip code, and availability. If we don’t have a zipper in your region yet, please contact Maze directly at (860) – 961 – 1820 and leave a voicemail with your contact information. Thank you for your support!

If you wish to contribute in other ways please fill out the form below or contact us MaskMakersInCT@gmail.com
